The question is: mass of aromatic hydrocarbons

Benzene is an aromatic hydrocarbon with molecular weight 78.1 (at one atmosphere pressure and 25°C, 1 ppm benzene is equivalent to 3.26 mg m−3). It occurs as a clear or colorless to light-yellow liquid with a gasoline-like odor.
